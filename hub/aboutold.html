Hello! I go by "the JMC" and I created jupitermornings.com to share the different projects I take on throughout my life. After trying to sort through my old photos from college, I realized that I did a lot of cool stuff then but I didn't have any other form of record of my experiences except those photos.

It got me thinking about how time, as far as I perceive it, keeps going forward and that the larger the time period between my past experiences and my current state grows, the less I remember. Taking pictures while living through those experiences only adds so much to the memories. I want more.

Thus, jupitermornings exists. Jupitermornings is the largest project I will be working on as it encompasses recording the progress of all the smaller projects. There is no goal other than to keep an extensive record of what I am doing in my life as it is happening. To do this, I will be utilizing as many mediums as necessary. In addition to this website, jupitermornings content is also available on Twitter, Instagram, and YouTube.

The projects I will be undertaking have been separated out into three categories: mental, emotional, and physical. You can read more about these in the "Projects" section below.
